# LoadQ — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-18_

LoadQ ("we", "us") operates the LoadQ mobile application (the "App") for
drivers and passengers coordinating shared inter-city loading queues. This
policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

## Information we collect

**Account information.** When you sign up we collect your name, email
address, and (where phone sign-in is enabled) phone number. Drivers also
provide date of birth, sex, and an optional profile photo.

**Vehicle information (drivers).** Make, model, year, plate number, colour,
and seat capacity of the vehicle(s) you register.

**Location.** With your permission, we use your device's GPS location to
detect the nearest loading zone, enforce zone geofencing when joining a
queue, and show nearby loading activity. Location is used while you use the
App; we do not track location in the background.

**Queue and trip activity.** Your position in queues, loading start/end
times, destination, seats filled, seat claims, and a history of loading
sessions and trips.

**Payment information.** Subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or
Google Play and managed via RevenueCat. We do **not** receive or store your
full payment card details. We receive subscription status (active, trial,
expired) only.

**Device & diagnostic data.** Basic technical data needed to operate and
debug the App (via our infrastructure providers).

## How we use it

- Operate the loading queue, matching, and history features
- Verify identity at sign-in (one-time codes by email/SMS)
- Enforce zone geofencing and the loading time window
- Provide trip history and analytics
- Manage subscriptions, trials, and access
- Maintain security and prevent abuse

## Service providers

We share data only with providers that help us run the App:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, file storage
- **RevenueCat** — subscription management
- **Apple / Google** — app distribution and in-app purchases
- **Expo** — app build and delivery infrastructure
- **Twilio / email provider** — sending one-time sign-in codes (when enabled)

We do not sell your personal information.

## Data retention

Account and vehicle data is kept while your account is active. Loading and
trip history is retained for operational and analytics purposes. You may
request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see
Contact).

## Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal
data by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable period.

## Children

LoadQ is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly
collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last
updated" date above.
